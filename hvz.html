Humans VS. Zombies Mobile

  • next
  • prev

During my stay at R.I.T. I learned what it was like to live in a zombie apocalypse. Welcome, to the ultimate game of tag played across campus. Stat tracking and mechanics were absent in the original. So lets fix that problem!

Survival Instinct

For a social game of this scope, Humans VS Zombies benefits from a mobile approach. By freeing users from their desktops and encouraging accesibility and teamwork Humans VS. Zombies becomes a whole new ordeal.



Research

To discover what players wanted most in Humans VS Zombies I reached out to the community. I interviewed use case studies with a short questionnaire and I was able to identify requested features and major pain points for the game.

Players unanimously wanted scanning and achievements in the game. While a good majority wanted social elements and a real time area map. Over all responses to the questions were positive and any additions to the original game would be a welcome change of pace.

Most Wanted Features

Scanning
96%
