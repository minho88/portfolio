The original class assignment was to redesign a pre-existing interface. I chose to redesign the Ford Focus's dash display system known as the MyFord Touch.

Onboard Design

I got the chance to interact and play around with the original MyFord Touch interface. From poor design to terrible navigation the original MyFord Touch displays were filled with clutter. Check out the original interface in my process work down below.



Research

In order to figure out what was objectively wrong with the MyFord Touch I decided to some user testing of my own. Conveniently there was a mockup program of the original MyFord Touch on the Google App Store meant to educate Ford Focus owners how to use their own technology.

For my testing purposes I set up a mockup car seat with the app a bit to right of the seat. A common complaint was the four quadrant setup. The users had difficulty reaching over to the lower right corner where the quick heating and cooling controls were. With this feedback I aimed to make everything easily within arms reach.
