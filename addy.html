Addy Awards Bumper

  • next
  • prev

The focus of this assignment was to create a bumper for the Addy Award Ceremonies which celebrated creativity. The bumper itself was made using a 3D modeling program, Cinema 4D and After Effects.

Crane Game

There was always a strange association paired with crane games that added a sense of excitement and tension. I wanted to recreate that feeling in my video. Adding to the sense of mystery behind the game was the looming uncertainty of the unknown.



Process

The environment was created as an open space to house the crane game. Using the phsyics engine in C4D I was able to stack the prizes in the machine.

Utilizing beautiful HDRIs the final outputs were awesome. After rendering in C4D I moved onto video editing in After Effects. With After Effects I tweaked the color and fixed some timing issues.
