Logo and Asset Design

The best part of the project was deciding together as a group what would be the best visual style to communicate our message. We decided on a simple and clean vector look after much deliberation.

My role in this phase was creating the logo and some assets for the game itself. The simplicity of the game appealed to our young demographic.

Interaction Design

During my research on interaction design I found that our users weren't interested in a nuanced control scheme. Taking simplicity into account I designed swipe based controls for the Ipads that could teach children the complexity of weather visually.

By playtesting the results the control methods were a huge success. They served both puposes of educating the user and visually representing the current state the game as a result.

Code and Animation

Working with our developers I ensured that the designs for the controls were spot on with the original. Maintaining updates with HTML/CSS/Javascript I was able to have both controllers ready for game day.

My last role in the project was to make sure the animations for our assets transitioned smoothly. Working around with the sprite sheets I made sure the game assets were pixel perfect for all scenarios.
